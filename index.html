Hanzi Deck is a Chinese character learning app designed to help you master strokes writing, memorize, and build vocabulary at your own pace and choice.

  • Character Strokes Writing
    Learn to write character stroke orders and composition.

  • Thousands of Available Characters
    Over 9574 simplified and traditional characters available in total.

  • Custom Decks
    Create your own decks and add entries with Chinese characters/words/sentence, pinyin, zhuyin, and definition.

  • Cloze Deletion
    You can also put certain characters inside a closure 【...】 in a sentence to only include those specific characters in the quiz.

  • Built-in HSK Decks
    Ready-to-use decks aligned with the HSK syllabus, both simplified and traditional, from HSK 1 to HSK 9.

  • Practice & Quiz Modes
    Learn through structured practice or test your knowledge with blind writing quizzes.

  • Voice
    Text-to-speech to learn pronunciation.

  • Dictionary
    Look up individual Chinese characters anytime with following information: pinyin, zhuyin (bopomofo), definition, radical, etymology, strokes animation, sound, and immediate practice mode.

  • Mastery Score
    Track your progress with a mastery score to see how well you’ve learned each character.

  • Balanced Mastery
    Adjust character frequency in each set of quiz based on your mastery score.

  • Decks Sharing
    Use the export-import feature to share decks.

  • Import from Anki
    Export your decks from Anki using .apkg files to import it in Hanzi Deck.

Simple, straightforward, and targeted for Mandarin learners at any level!
